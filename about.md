**Sapienism** is a _school of thought._ According to **Wikipedia,** a school of thought is defined as:  

>> "_a collection or group of people who share common characteristics of opinion or outlook of a philosophy, discipline, belief, social movement, economics, cultural movement, or art movement._"

Sapienism has three fundamental principles:  

* Act Logically
* Adapt Rapidly
* Evolve Infinitely
